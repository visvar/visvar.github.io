Franziska Becker, M.Sc.

I'm a visualization researcher, originally from cognitive science, and study interaction and sensemaking in visualization contexts.

I did my Bachelor’s degree in cognitive science and then completed my Master’s degree in computer science, both at the University of Osnabrück in Germany. Right now, I am close to finishing my Ph.D. at the University of Stuttgart, under supervision of Thomas Ertl.

Research Interests

  • Visualization & visual analytics
  • Externalization
  • HCI
  • Game Design
