Fairouz Grioui, M.Sc.

My research focuses on the study of small-scale visualizations on wearable devices along with interaction techniques for mobile contexts.

I completed my Master’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Paris-Saclay in France. Currently, I am pursuing my Ph.D. at the University of Stuttgart. In my previous work I explored micro visualization reading under locomotion such as while walking and jogging(opens in new tab) or playing a tennis-like game in VR(opens in new tab)), and in motion to assist patients with (wrist rehabilitation exercises at home(opens in new tab)).

Research Interests

  • Mobile Visualization
  • Information Visualization
  • HCI
