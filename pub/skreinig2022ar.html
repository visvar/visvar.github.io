AR Hero: Generating Interactive Augmented Reality Guitar Tutorials

Authors. Lucchas Ribeiro Skreinig, Ana Stanescu, Shohei Mori, Frank Heyen, Peter Mohr, Michael Sedlmair, Dieter Schmalstieg, Denis Kalkofen
Venue. VRW (2022)
Materials. DOI PDF
Abstract. We introduce a system capable of generating interactive Augmented Reality guitar tutorials by parsing common digital guitar tablature and by capturing the performance of an expert using a multi-camera array. Instructions are presented to the user in an Augmented Reality application using either an abstract visualization, a 3D virtual hand, or a 3D video. To support individual users at different skill levels the system provides full control of the playback of a tutorial, including its speed and looping behavior, while delivering live feedback on the userâ€™s performance.
